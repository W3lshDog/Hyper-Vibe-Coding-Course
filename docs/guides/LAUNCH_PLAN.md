## FAQ - COMMON QUESTIONS

### "Can I delay the course launch?"

**Answer:** Yes. The 4-week timeline is aggressive but doable. If you need more time:
- Move video recording to Week 1-2
- Add Week 0 (soft launch to friends)
- Do "beta cohort" with 10 friends first

### "Do I need to record professional videos?"

**Answer:** No. Authentic > polished. Use Loom (shows your screen + face). Your enthusiasm matters more than production quality.

### "Can I run without Discord?"

**Answer:** Technically yes, but Discord is free and makes community 10x better. Highly recommended. Alternative: Slack (free tier) or Facebook Group.

### "What if no one signs up?"

**Answer:** This is normal. Most courses get 0 signups the first week. Focus on:
- Keep posting on Reddit + Twitter
- Ask friends to share
- Improve landing page copy (A/B test)
- Email your existing network

By week 4, you should have 30-50 signups.

### "Can I change the curriculum after launch?"

**Answer:** Absolutely. In fact, you should. Gather feedback week 1-2, iterate week 3-4. Best courses evolve.

### "Do I need to be an expert?"

**Answer:** No. You're 1-2 weeks ahead of students. That's enough. Be honest: "I'm learning too" builds trust.
