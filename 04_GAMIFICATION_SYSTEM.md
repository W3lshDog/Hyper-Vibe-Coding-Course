## PART 4: LEADERBOARD SETUP

### Leaderboard Rules

```
SCORING:
- Points are primary metric
- Ties broken by: Submissions per week (consistency)
- Then by: Peer votes (quality over quantity)

RESETS:
- Weekly leaderboard (resets Monday)
- Course leaderboard (permanent until course ends)
- All-time leaderboard (permanent forever)

PRIVACY:
- Opt-in only (ask permission before listing)
- Show display name OR first name + last initial
- Allow "Anonymous" option

TIERS:
🥇 Gold (Top 10%)
🥈 Silver (Top 25%)
```

## PART 8: GAMIFICATION PSYCHOLOGY

### Why This System Works

**1. IMMEDIATE FEEDBACK**
- Students see points instantly
- Badges appear in Discord
- Progress bar updates

→ Dopamine hit every day

**2. MULTIPLE WIN CONDITIONS**
- Can't win on points alone
- Can win on community (mentor badge)
- Can win on quality (polish badge)
- Can win on speed (early submission bonus)

→ Different students succeed in different ways

**3. PROGRESSION VISIBILITY**
- See current level
- Know exactly what's needed for next level
- Leaderboard shows peer progress

→ Intrinsic motivation (not just extrinsic points)

**4. SOCIAL PROOF**
- Badge announcements are PUBLIC
- Leaderboard shows who's winning
- "Your friend just earned X badge"

→ FOMO + community pride

**5. OPTIONAL COMPETITIVENESS**
- Leaderboard is opt-in
- No shaming for lower scores
- Focus is on personal progress

→ Motivation without stress
